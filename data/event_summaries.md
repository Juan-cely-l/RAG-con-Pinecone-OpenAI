# UFC Event Summaries (Sample Reference)

This file includes short event summaries for a local RAG demo corpus.

## UFC 229 (short summary)

UFC 229 is widely remembered for the lightweight title fight between Khabib Nurmagomedov and Conor McGregor. The event drew major attention because of the rivalry, the pre-fight build-up, and the high stakes of the championship bout.

## UFC 268 (short summary)

UFC 268 featured a welterweight title fight between Kamaru Usman and Colby Covington and a strawweight title fight between Rose Namajunas and Zhang Weili. It is often referenced for its high-level competition and significant divisional implications.

## UFC 300 (short summary)

UFC 300 was discussed as a milestone numbered event and was promoted as a major card. Fans and media often referenced the event for its symbolic importance and the expectation of a strong fight lineup.
