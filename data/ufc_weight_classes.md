# UFC Weight Classes (Sample Reference)

This file is a short educational summary of common UFC weight divisions for a RAG demo project.

## Men's divisions (commonly referenced)

- Flyweight: up to 125 lb
- Bantamweight: up to 135 lb
- Featherweight: up to 145 lb
- Lightweight: up to 155 lb
- Welterweight: up to 170 lb
- Middleweight: up to 185 lb
- Light Heavyweight: up to 205 lb
- Heavyweight: up to 265 lb

## Women's divisions (commonly referenced)

- Strawweight: up to 115 lb
- Flyweight: up to 125 lb
- Bantamweight: up to 135 lb
- Featherweight: up to 145 lb (used less frequently than other divisions)

## Notes

- Fighters usually weigh in before the event and then rehydrate before competition.
- A bout can be cancelled or changed if a fighter misses weight and the promotion does not approve a catchweight or alternative arrangement.
- Weight classes exist to promote competitive fairness and reduce mismatch risk.

This document is intentionally compact so it can be indexed as part of a small local RAG corpus.
