# Fighter Summaries (Sample Reference)

This file contains short, high-level summaries of well-known MMA fighters for retrieval examples.

## Jon Jones

Jon Jones is known for his long reach, creative striking, clinch work, and strong wrestling skills. Across multiple title runs, he built a reputation for adapting to different opponents and controlling range.

## Georges St-Pierre

Georges St-Pierre (GSP) is often cited as one of the most complete mixed martial artists of his era. He combined disciplined jab-based striking with elite timing on takedowns and a strong game plan approach.

## Khabib Nurmagomedov

Khabib Nurmagomedov became famous for pressure grappling, chain wrestling, top control, and ground-and-pound. His style often forced opponents backward and limited their ability to establish offense.

## Amanda Nunes

Amanda Nunes is known for powerful striking, finishing ability, and the ability to dictate exchanges early. She is frequently discussed in conversations about the greatest women's MMA fighters.

## Valentina Shevchenko

Valentina Shevchenko is recognized for technical striking, timing, and tactical control. Her background and calm fight management are often highlighted in analyst breakdowns.

These summaries are intentionally short and are meant for demo retrieval, not full biographies.
